GGSSS SALWAN

GGSSS SALWAN was established in 1952 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in ASSANDH block of KARNAL district of HARYANA. The school consists of Grades from 6 to 12. The school is Girls and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Not Applicable in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Hindi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
